Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Ezy Photo Clean ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Ezy Photo Clean mobile application (the "App").

Privacy First: Ezy Photo Clean is designed with your privacy as our top priority. All photo analysis happens entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your photos, videos, or personal data on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information We DO NOT Collect

Ezy Photo Clean is built to respect your privacy. We DO NOT:

  • Access, collect, or store your photos or videos
  • Track your location or GPS coordinates
  • Collect personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Access your contacts, messages, or other apps
  • Transmit any data to external servers

1.2 On-Device Processing Only

All photo and video analysis occurs entirely on your device using Apple's PhotoKit framework. This includes:

  • Duplicate photo detection
  • Blur detection
  • Screenshot identification
  • Large file analysis
  • Location classification (using EXIF metadata embedded in your photos)

The analysis results never leave your device and are stored only in your device's local cache.

1.3 Purchase Information

When you subscribe to Ezy Photo Clean Premium, your purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store. We receive only:

  • Subscription status (active/inactive)
  • Product identifier (monthly or yearly subscription)
  • Transaction verification tokens (for security)

Apple handles all payment processing, and we never have access to your credit card information, billing address, or other payment details. Please review Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how Apple processes your payment data.

2. How We Use Information

The limited information we receive is used solely to:

  • Verify subscription status: Confirm that you have an active premium subscription
  • Provide premium features: Unlock unlimited cleaning, smart insights, and other premium functionality
  • Prevent fraud: Validate purchase receipts to prevent unauthorized access

3. Photo Library Access

Ezy Photo Clean requests access to your Photo Library to perform its core functions:

  • Read Access: To analyze your photos and videos for duplicates, quality issues, and organization opportunities
  • Write Access: To delete photos and videos that you explicitly choose to remove

Important: Photo Library access is required for the app to function. You can revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos > Ezy Photo Clean. However, the app will not function without this permission.

3.1 What We Do With Photo Access

  • Scan metadata (size, date, resolution, location from EXIF)
  • Generate thumbnails for duplicate detection
  • Analyze image quality (blur detection)
  • Identify duplicate videos
  • Delete photos/videos you select for removal

3.2 What We DON'T Do With Photo Access

  • Upload photos or videos to our servers (we don't have servers)
  • Share photos with third parties
  • Use photos for advertising or marketing
  • Train AI/ML models on your photos
  • Perform facial recognition beyond on-device processing

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage Only

All app data is stored locally on your device:

  • Cache: Temporary thumbnails and analysis results stored in app cache
  • Preferences: App settings stored in iOS UserDefaults
  • Database: Scan results stored in on-device CoreData database

4.2 Data Protection

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS sandboxing (data isolated from other apps)
  • Device encryption (when device passcode/Face ID is enabled)
  • No network transmission (data never leaves your device)

4.3 Data Deletion

You can delete all app data by:

  • Uninstalling the app (removes all cached data)
  • Clearing app cache in iOS Settings > General > iPhone Storage > Ezy Photo Clean

Ezy Photo Clean uses the following Apple services:

  • StoreKit: For in-app purchase processing (managed by Apple)

  • CoreLocation: For reverse geocoding location names from photo EXIF data (on-device only)

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
